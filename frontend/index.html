Frequently Asked Questions

How does TripPilot AI work?

+

We use an advanced LangGraph architecture with specialized AI agents. You provide constraints, and our agents (Budget, Hotel, Itinerary, etc.) negotiate and build a comprehensive plan.

Is it free?

+

We offer a generous free tier that allows up to 3 complete trip generations per month. Pro users get unlimited trips, PDF exports, and live API hotel booking integrations.

Can I export itineraries?

+

Yes! Once an itinerary is generated, you can save it to your dashboard, export it as a highly formatted PDF, or sync it directly to Google Calendar.

Does it support international travel?

+

Absolutely. TripPilot AI covers over 190 countries and automatically includes visa requirements and local custom tips via our Travel Advisor Agent.
